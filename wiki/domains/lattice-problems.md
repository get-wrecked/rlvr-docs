# Lattice Problems (Cryptographic)

## Overview
Solve lattice problems: shortest vector (SVP), closest vector (CVP), lattice reduction (LLL/BKZ). Verify via norm bounds.

## Verification Mechanism
Verify found vector is in lattice (integer linear combination of basis). Verify norm is within Hermite factor of optimal.
