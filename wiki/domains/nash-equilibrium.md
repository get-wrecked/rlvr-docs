# Nash Equilibrium Computation

## Overview
Compute Nash equilibria for normal-form and extensive-form games. Verify via best-response condition: no player can improve by unilateral deviation.

## Verification Mechanism
For each player, verify no alternative strategy yields higher payoff given others' strategies. Use Lemke-Howson or support enumeration for reference.
