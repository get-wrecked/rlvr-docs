# Clinical Trial Design

## Overview
Design clinical trials: sample size calculation, randomization scheme, endpoint selection, statistical analysis plan. Verify via power analysis and regulatory compliance checks.

## Verification Mechanism
Verify sample size achieves target power (>80%) via simulation. Check randomization produces balanced groups. Verify primary endpoint is measurable. Check against FDA/EMA guidance.
